The Ground Under AEC Is Moving. Leading Through It Is the Work.

75%
AEC Industry Data

of AEC firms now use AI, up roughly 20 points in a single year.

The report's title says it plainly: the industry is now competing at the speed of change.

Source · Unanet · 2026 AEC Inspire Report

Three forces are reshaping the AEC landscape at once, technology, consolidation, and ownership. Each is a change to be led, not just absorbed.

The pace of change in AEC has stopped being an occasional event and become the operating environment. Unanet's 2026 AEC Inspire Report, subtitled Winning at the Speed of Change, found AI adoption jumping to roughly three in four firms in a single year, even as only 29% say they have high confidence in the data feeding those tools. The tools are moving faster than the trust in them.

At the same time, deal activity keeps consolidating the industry. Zweig Group's monthly transaction reports show a steady drumbeat of acquisitions across architecture, engineering, and construction, reshaping who owns what and how firms compete. And underneath it all sits a generational ownership shift, with new owners taking on larger stakes and firms rethinking how leadership passes from one generation to the next.

None of these forces is slowing down. What separates firms that thrive from firms that stall is rarely the change itself. It's whether leaders name it early, bring people with them, and stay clear about what stays constant while everything else moves.

Firm Update · Strategic Partnership

A Strategic Partnership, Growing Stronger Together.

An update from Jeff Adams on our strategic partnership with Zweig Group. The SN and ZG M&A teams continue to strengthen how they collaborate, through operational alignment, shared technology, and joint business development. Here's where things stand.

One Client Experience

Co-branded SN/ZG proposals, marketing materials, and service templates now give clients one clear view of the capabilities available across the partnership, spanning sell-side advisory, buy-side advisory, valuation, and transaction readiness, for a more consistent experience that reflects the full depth of what each firm brings.

Aligned Operations

Weekly M&A calls keep both teams in sync, sharing best practices and standardizing methodology across proposal development, pricing, buyer outreach, transaction execution, and client service, with transparency and knowledge-sharing across both teams.

One Technology Platform

We've adopted MadeMarket as our shared M&A engagement platform and are migrating to a single system, improving visibility across opportunities, bringing consistency to pipeline and workflows, and positioning the shared platform for scalable growth.
